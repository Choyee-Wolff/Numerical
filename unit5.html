Collaborative Discussion 1

In this task, I emplored opinions on intrapartum care among GPs by grouping similar responses to simplify the data and make trends clearer. This approach helped reduce complexity, making the findings easier to interpret.

I created two different bar charts to compare which visualisation best represents the data. The stacked bar chart (plot 1) shows the overall distribution of responses within each category, while the dodged bar chart (plot 2) allows for easier comparison of individual response categories across statements. I found that the right chart is clearer for general audiences as it allows for direct comparisons, but I personally prefer the left chart for showing overall trends. The choice of visualisation depends on the audience.
