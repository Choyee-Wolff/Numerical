Baye's Probability Activity

1. A fair die with faces numbered from 1 to 6 is rolled. Calculate the probability that the die lands with an even number uppermost?

P(even number) = 0.5

2. Coronary heart disease (CHD) deaths in the UK in 2002

P(Male dies of CHD) = 64,473/288,332 = 0.2236

P(Female dies of CHD) = 53,003/318,463 = 0.1665

How accurate are your estimates likely to be?

The estimates are reasonably accurate for that specific year (2002) but may not hold true in the future due to various health, societal, and technological changes.
